Our Story

From one matting pitch to a complete cricket ecosystem

YL Cricket Academy began in 2011 with a simple idea: give every child in our community access to proper cricket coaching, regardless of where they were starting from. What started as weekend sessions on a single matting pitch has grown into a full academy with turf grounds, analysis labs and a coaching staff of 24.

Today we run programs for players from age 6 to 19, an elite performance pathway for serious competitors, and a packed calendar of local matches, district tournaments and seasonal tours.
